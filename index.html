Lumieron - Tutoring in Math and Reading

Welcome to Lumieron – throwing light on learning reading and math.

Serving Pittsburgh’s South Hills for grades 3 through middle school.

We tutor students using natural, skill-based techniques that exclude rote or mechanical learning.
